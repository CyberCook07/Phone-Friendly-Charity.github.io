The Second Wind Foundation

What is the Second Wind Foundation?

The Second Wind Foundation is a charity that was made to help athletes of any age to be able to gain a second wind from any injuries that could harm and or potientially end their atheltic career. Every game comes with a risk that athletes have to face which is injuries some are more severe than others. The most heart renching thing to watch is a atheletes multiple years of hard work and dedication go down the drain in an instant due to them moving either too fast, turning too quick, or even doing a basic everyday move. 60% of athletes sustained servere injuries just through college and 32% of athletes are forced to retire due to injuries.

Second Wind wants to give those athletes a chance to come back better and more powerful. We don't want athletes to see these injuries as the end of their career but as a chance to build a story of coming back from devistating injuries better than ever. At Second Wind we want to see 32% of career ending injuries to athletes drop. It's not fair to the athlete and their time and to the sport that they have dedicated their life to practicing and loving.
